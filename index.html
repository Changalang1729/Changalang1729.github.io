Yale University

Computer Science (BS) & Chem (BS), 16-20



Yale University’s renowned history speaks for itself — yet, what surprises me most is that Yale’s magnificence has little to do with its prestige. No, instead, it is truly the people that comprise Yale’s mystique. From Yale’s incredibly distinguished faculty and the caring residential college staff to its eclectic and brilliant student body, transformative ideas bounce around Yale’s comfortable atmosphere like molecules in a high temperature system. I look forward to another year at this wonderful institution.
